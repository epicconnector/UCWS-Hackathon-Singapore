## Event Details

**Q: When is the hackathon happening?**  
A: The Grand Finale & Pitch Day is on **June 13, 2026** in Singapore. The online coding phase runs for 30-60 days from April onwards.

**Q: Is the hackathon online or offline?**  
A: It's a hybrid event with:
- **Online phase:** April-June (30-60 days of coding)
- **Offline finale:** June 13, 2026 in Singapore (Grand Finale & Pitch Day)

**Q: Is there a participation fee?**  
A: Please check the official registration page for pricing and fee information.

**Q: Do I need to be in Singapore for the entire event?**  
A: The online coding phase is completely remote. Only the final pitch day on June 13 requires in-person participation in Singapore.

## Tracks & Prizes

**Q: How many competition tracks are there?**  
A: There are 3 tracks:
1. **Main Track - Free Building Track** ($10,000 USD prize pool)
2. **MiroMind Special Track** ($10,000 USD prize pool)
3. **Additional Track** ($10,000 USD prize pool)

**Q: What is the MiroMind Special Track?**  
A: This track is dedicated to innovative applications leveraging MiroMind technology. Check the registration page for technical specifications.

**Q: What does the $10,000 prize pool include?**  
A: Each track's $10,000 prize pool includes prizes, cash rewards, and token mix value distributed among winning teams.

**Q: Can I submit to multiple tracks?**  
A: Please check the official rules and guidelines on the registration page.
