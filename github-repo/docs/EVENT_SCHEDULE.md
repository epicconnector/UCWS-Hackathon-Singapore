## Pre-Hackathon Phase (April 2026)

### Claw Tour 2026 - Warming Events

EPICConnector is organizing a 4-city, 4-week tour to warmup and prepare for the hackathon:

- **Cities:** Singapore, Hong Kong, Shanghai, London
- **Format:** Weekly events on Saturdays
- **Purpose:** Networking, learning, team formation, and preparation

**What to Expect:**
- Keynote speakers and industry leaders
- Workshops and technical sessions
- Networking opportunities
- Team formation sessions
- Sponsor showcases
- Product launch discussions

**How to Participate:**
👉 [Register for Claw Tour Events](https://luma.com/user/epic_connector)

### Phase 2: Online Coding Phase
**Timeline:** April - June 2026 (30-60 days)

- 🔨 Regular check-ins and updates
- 🔨 Community support via WhatsApp/Discord

**Key Dates:**

- **Week 3-4:** Feature development
- **Week 5-6:** Testing and refinement

**What to Do:**
- Develop your AI-native product
- Follow project submission guidelines
- Test your application thoroughly
- Prepare documentation and demos
- Connect with the community for feedback

### Phase 3: Final Submission
**Timeline:** Late May 2026

- 📋 Final project submission through platform
- 📋 Code submission (optional: GitHub repository)
- 📋 Demo video preparation

- 📋 Team presentation preparation

- Any additional resources

### Phase 4: Grand Finale & Pitch Day
**Date:** June 13, 2026  
**Location:** Singapore (In-person)

#### Schedule Overview

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 08:00 - 09:00 | Registration & Breakfast |
| 09:00 - 09:30 | Opening Ceremony & Keynotes |
| 09:30 - 12:00 | Main Track Pitches (Free Building) |
| 12:00 - 13:00 | Lunch Break |
| 13:00 - 15:30 | MiroMind Track Pitches |
| 15:30 - 15:45 | Coffee Break |
| 15:45 - 17:00 | Additional Track Pitches |
| 17:00 - 17:30 | Judging & Deliberation |
| 17:30 - 18:30 | Awards Ceremony & Celebration |
| 18:30 - 20:00 | Networking & Closing Reception |

#### What to Expect

**Presentations:**
- Each team gets **5-7 minutes** to pitch
- Q&A session with judges (**3-5 minutes**)
- Live demonstrations of your product

**Judging Criteria:**
- Innovation and creativity
- Technical implementation
- User experience and design
- Business potential and scalability
- Presentation and communication
- Impact and problem-solving

**Judges:**
- Industry leaders and investors
- AI/Tech entrepreneurs
- EPICConnector community leaders
- Previous hackathon winners

**Awards Ceremony:**
- Top 3 winners per track announced
- Prize distributions and token mix
- Winner announcements and celebrations
- Photo opportunities
- Media coverage
